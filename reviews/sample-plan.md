# AI Tools for Accountants — Lunch & Learn

**Author:** Maria (owner, 15-person accounting firm)
**Format:** one 45-minute session, whole team, no follow-up currently scheduled
**Audience context given to Andra:** all 15 team members, mixed AI familiarity (a
couple have used ChatGPT personally, most haven't touched it). The actual task Maria
wants Claude picking up: drafting the routine "here's what changed in your filing this
year" client memos sent after every completed return.

---

## Agenda

**1. What is Claude? (10 min)**
Brief overview of Claude as an AI assistant — what large language models are, how they
generate text, and why they're useful for knowledge work.

**2. How LLMs work (10 min)**
A simplified explanation of how models like Claude are trained and how they generate
responses, so the team has some intuition for why outputs can vary.

**3. Prompting basics + live demo (15 min)**
I'll demo writing a good prompt live — show a before/after of a rough prompt vs. a
well-structured one, using a generic example memo.

**4. Q&A (10 min)**
Open floor for questions.

**Closing line (last slide):** "Soon the team won't have to spend hours writing routine
client memos by hand."
